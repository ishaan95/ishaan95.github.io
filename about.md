Welcome to my website! I'm a PhD student in the [Computational Media](https://grad.soe.ucsc.edu/computational-media) department at the [Jack Baskin School of Engineering](https://www.soe.ucsc.edu/) at [University of California, Santa Cruz](https://www.ucsc.edu/). I’m working on verification of autonomous vehicle software in simulation. Our team has been working on procedural (algorithmic) road network and intersection generation as well as AI vehicle and pedestrian simulation agents in Unreal Engine. Using a game engine to create scenes with the aforementioned components allows us to verify functionality for the perception as well as motion planning stacks of AVs. We have also been working on methods to create scenarios procedurally to effectively test autonomous vehicles.

I’m committed to creating a positive, meaningful impact to the autonomous, intelligent transportation communities as well as associated communities such as robotics, software verification and digital games. 

I enjoy teaching and have been involved as a TA for courses ranging from computer architecture, networks to game design and have been an instructor for Game Technologies, offered in Summer 2020 at UCSC.

A major strength of mine is the ability to work in teams. Currently, our team at the Augmented Design Lab includes graduate as well as undergraduate students.
